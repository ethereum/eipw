markdown-requires-ref-body

Proposals listed in the `requires` field must be mentioned somewhere in the body of the document.

Examples

error[markdown-requires-ref-body]: proposals EIP-5 must be mentioned in the body
  --> test-eip.md:11:11
   |
11 | requires: 4, 5, 6
   |           ^^^^^^^ required here
   |
error[markdown-requires-ref-body]: proposals EIP-5, EIP-6, EIP-7 must be mentioned in the body
  --> test-eip.md:11:11
   |
11 | requires: 4, 5, 6, 7
   |           ^^^^^^^^^^ required here
   |

Explanation

markdown-requires-ref-body ensures that any proposals listed in the preamble `requires` field are actually mentioned somewhere in the body text of the document. This helps catch cases where required proposals are listed but not discussed.

The lint recognizes both EIP-X and ERC-X format references in the body text.
